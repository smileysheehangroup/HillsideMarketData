Understanding the Risk
Why Builder Offers Are Not Market Value
🏗️

A builder's offer is calculated very differently than a buyer's offer

When a builder contacts you, their math starts with: What can we sell a new construction home for? Then they subtract demolition costs, construction costs, carrying costs, and profit margin. Your home's charm, history, and livability don't factor in — only the lot dimensions do.

  • New construction in Hillside (75214) is listing for $1.5M–$5.8M
  • Pre-1965 homes are selling for $480,000–$1,040,000 on the retail market
  • Builders typically need to buy land at a large discount to make their numbers work
  • Hillside has fewer teardowns than most Dallas neighborhoods — preservation is a community value here
